About Me

Hello! My name is Shunhua Fu, and I am currently a Computer Science major at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. Colloquially, I just go by Sam. I am into trying out new things and being spontaneous. In my free time, I like to run and play tennis, as well as dance (pop and locking). I've done lots of cool things, so feel free to stay awhile and look around.

I enjoy Computer Science because of the possibilities that lie just beyond my fingertips. It is refreshing that I am the only limiting factor in my programs; what my code can and cannot do is only limited by my imagination. I especially like back end development and writing algorithms because they are no different than puzzles like Rubik's Cubes and Gundam models. Besides, people think I'm a wizard.
